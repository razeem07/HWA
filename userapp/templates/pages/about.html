About Eleister Med

Bringing Excellence in Healthcare from India to Dubai

Our Story

Delivering World Class Healthcare Excellence

ELISTER MED brings the trusted Indian healthcare excellence to Dubai, offering comprehensive patient-centered medical care under one roof. With our rich heritage of providing exceptional medical services in India, we have expanded our reach to serve the diverse community of Dubai.

Our superspeciality clinic offers 360° medical services, combining expert physicians, cutting-edge diagnostics, and patient-first care. We are committed to providing quality healthcare that is accessible, affordable, and compassionate.

At ELISTER MED, every patient is treated as family. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with modern technology and staffed by internationally trained medical professionals who are dedicated to your health and well-being.

What Drives Us

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To provide world-class healthcare services that are accessible, affordable, and compassionate. We strive to enhance the quality of life for our patients through comprehensive medical care and advanced treatment options.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred healthcare provider in Dubai, recognized for clinical excellence, patient satisfaction, and innovation in medical care. We envision a healthier community through preventive and curative healthcare.

Our Values

We uphold the highest standards of integrity, compassion, and excellence. Our values include patient-centered care, continuous improvement, teamwork, respect for all, and commitment to ethical medical practices.
