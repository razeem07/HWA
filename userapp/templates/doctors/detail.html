About Dr. Ananya Nair

Dr. Ananya Nair completed her MBBS from a reputed medical university and later specialized in Internal Medicine (MD). She pursued super-specialty training in Cardiology (DM) with a focus on interventional cardiac procedures.

With years of clinical experience, Dr. Ananya has successfully handled complex cardiac interventions including angioplasty and coronary stenting. She is well known for her precision, modern treatment methods, and patient safety standards.

She actively participates in cardiology research programs and international conferences to stay updated with advanced heart care techniques. She is also passionate about patient awareness and preventive cardiac care.
